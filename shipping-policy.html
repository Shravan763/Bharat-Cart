Shipping Policy

Everything you need to know about our fast deliveries.

1. Processing Time: All orders are processed within 24 hours. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays.


2. Shipping Rates & Delivery Estimates: Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout. We offer Free Express Shipping on all prepaid and Cash on Delivery (COD) orders. Delivery delays can occasionally occur, but standard delivery is between 2-5 business days.


3. Shipment Confirmation & Order Tracking: You will receive a Shipment Confirmation email/SMS once your order has shipped containing your tracking number(s). The tracking number will be active within 24 hours.


4. Damages: BharatCart is liable for any products damaged or lost during shipping. If you received your order damaged, please contact us immediately to file a claim and get a replacement.
